Mountain Lion

Also known as puma, panther, cougar, and catamount is the largest wildcat in North America.

  • Scientific Name: Puma concolor
  • Speed: 40-50 mph (Running)
  • Average Lifespan: 8-13 years (In the wild)
  • Habitat: Americas

Found mostly in western U.S., mountain lions are hunters who prey primarily on deers but also coyotes, raccoons, and other smaller animals. Their strong legs give them the ability to jump as far as 40 to 45 feet and they run as fast as 40 to 50 mph.
